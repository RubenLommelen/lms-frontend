Evolve&Go stands for making progress(Evolve) & putting all the knowledge into practice(GO).

With Evolve&Go we strive to simplify the learning progress during the SwitchFully track.

  • As a student you can keep track of your codelab progress and help each other out by sharing solutions.
  • As a coach we you can have an overview of each students progress.

This application is developed by Alperen, Baker, Rinaldo & Ruben with the guidance of the SwitchFully coaches. A special thanks to Colruyt-Group for providing us with this unique opportunity.
